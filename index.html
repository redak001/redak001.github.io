Reanna Edakkara is a board-certfied doctor with over 10 of experience in Pediatrics. Known for her extensive research in Pedaitric Oncology, Reanna hopes to be one step closer to finding a cure for Leukemia. She has treated less fortunate patients in South Africa and India. Her devotion to the medical field is only strengthened by her dedication to her patients. Reanna holds a Bachelors of Science in Sociology, from University of California, Riverside, where she developed a strong foundation in health equity and public health. Throughout her career, she has worked with a diverse range of patients. This experience has equipped her with a versatile skill set and the ability to adapt to various challenges.By treating patients from all over the world, Reanna was able to learn about new diseases and conditions to help other kids. Prior to her current position, Reanna held roles at Children's Hospital, Los Angeles, where she worked alongside Dr. Kenneth Lee to bring stem cell therapy to pedatric patients. Her work in stem cell therapy not only contributed saving the lives of many of her patients but also highlighted her ability to collaborate with her peers in order to ensure that children with luekemia have a higher chance at surviving the disease.
